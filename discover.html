Discover UMD

About the University of Minnesota Duluth

I'm proud to be a student at the University of Minnesota Duluth (UMD), where I'm pursuing my Communications major with a Marketing minor. UMD offers a vibrant campus community in a beautiful location on the shores of Lake Superior.

My Experience at UMD

Being at UMD has given me incredible opportunities to grow both academically and personally. The Communications program has helped me develop strong storytelling skills and understand the power of effective communication in marketing and media.

The campus environment, combined with the natural beauty of Duluth, makes it an inspiring place to learn and create. I've been able to apply what I learn in the classroom to real-world projects, especially in social media strategy and content creation.
