Hobbies

Park Point & Lake Life

I absolutely love Park Point beach. If you go on just the right day, the sun is shining and the waves on Lake Superior are crashing in and it almost feels like you are at the ocean. The water is crystal clear and super refreshing.

I have gone to the beach many times with my friends and my boyfriend. One of our favorite activities to do there is play bucket golf!

I also love to be at my lake place and spend quality time with my family, whether it's sitting by a campfire or watching the young kids play kickball, I always find so much joy in being there.

I also love to hike, whether it's with my roommates or my boyfriend, it's a great way to get good exercise and see the beautiful outdoors. One of my favorite memories is hiking around Gooseberry Falls! It's the little things in life that bring me so much joy and remind me of how amazing life can be.

Other Activities

When I'm not outdoors I enjoy reading, trying out new recipes, and creating content for social media.
